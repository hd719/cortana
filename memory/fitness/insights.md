
## Dry January 2026 - Recovery Correlation

**Observation:** Hamel did Dry January (no alcohol for the entire month)

**Result:** ZERO red recovery days in January
- All days were green (>66%) or yellow (34-66%)
- No days below 34% recovery

**Insight:** Alcohol abstinence correlates strongly with consistently better recovery scores. This is a powerful baseline to compare against months with alcohol consumption.
