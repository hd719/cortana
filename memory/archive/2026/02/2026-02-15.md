### Backtesting Engine Built (Python)
Location: `~/Desktop/services/backtester/`

**Created from scratch:**
- `config.py` — loads Alpaca keys
- `indicators.py` — RSI, SMA, EMA, MACD, Bollinger, ATR, relative strength
- `backtest.py` — core engine with equity tracking, trade logging, metrics
