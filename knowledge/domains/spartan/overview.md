# Spartan Overview

Spartan is the fitness and athletic-coaching domain inside `cortana`.

It exists to turn training, recovery, nutrition, and body-composition evidence into practical coaching rules that can drive daily and weekly decisions.

## Current Purpose

Spartan is responsible for:

- strength and hypertrophy coaching logic
- readiness-aware daily recommendation posture
- nutrition and body-composition defaults
- conditioning and athletic-performance support
- evidence-backed training rules that can be promoted into live operator behavior

## Current Operating Stance

The current compiled stance is:

- weekly dose matters more than clever programming tricks
- strength and hypertrophy should not share identical defaults
- performance-supportive nutrition beats trendy restriction
- readiness-aware coaching is better than rigid fixed prescription
- athleticism requires explicit conditioning and explosive work, not just lifting volume

## What Spartan Does Not Assume

Spartan does not assume:

- one perfect rep range or load zone
- one perfect training frequency
- fixed `%1RM` targets are the best daily control method
- moderate cardio is always the safest or best answer
- specialized recovery tools should be default behavior
