# Spartan Current State

The Spartan domain is active as a planning and research-backed coaching layer, with runtime execution split across `cortana` and `cortana-external`.

## Repo Role

`cortana` currently owns:

- Spartan planning and operator docs
- the raw and derived Spartan research corpus
- the compiled Spartan knowledge layer
- identity and coaching doctrine for the Spartan lane

`cortana-external` currently owns:

- the runtime-facing provider and service surfaces
- health and fitness integrations such as Whoop and Tonal
- the local service endpoints that feed Spartan artifacts

## Current Operating Posture

The current live posture is:

- readiness-driven daily recommendations
- nutrition and data-coverage gaps lower confidence instead of always suppressing training
- Apple Health is optional for now and should not break the core coaching loop
- the system is stronger at interpretation and recommendation than at fully adaptive programming

## Current Strengths

Right now Spartan is strongest at:

- daily readiness interpretation
- daily and weekly artifact generation
- provider-backed training context from Whoop and Tonal
- evidence-backed reasoning about load, frequency, nutrition, and fatigue

## Current Limits

Right now Spartan is still limited by:

- uneven nutrition and body-composition coverage
- incomplete sleep-specific evidence in the current research corpus
- the absence of mature live velocity instrumentation for true VBT logic
- the gap between good research synthesis and fully automated programming decisions
